Journal of counseling psychology · Belief, bond, and relief: Longitudinal associations between client belief about counseling success, working alliance, and symptom relief.

檢驗自然隱喻模式在培養伴侶親密感、接納、關係滿意度、自我慈悲與正向溝通之成效

Journal of marital and family therapy · Examining the Effectiveness of Nature Metaphor Patterns for Cultivating Intimacy, Acceptance, Relationship Satisfaction, Self-Compassion, and Positive Communication in Couples.

#3 ⚖️ 低實用性

整合焦慮與動機：調節接近-退縮衝突的前額葉機制

Neuroscience and biobehavioral reviews · Integrating Anxiety and Motivation: Prefrontal Mechanisms Arbitrating the Approach-Avoidance Conflict.

焦慮與動機共享神經迴路，低至中度的焦慮能促進目標追求，但持續的高焦慮會透過破壞前額葉調控機制，導致行為偏向退縮與動機減損，此非線性關係為臨床上理解個案「想改變卻又逃避」提供了神經科學基礎。
